# GymOS Module Documentation

## Staff Management
- Add staff with name, role, phone, email, shift, salary, status
- Filter by role and status
- Edit and delete staff
- Stats: total, active, trainers, avg salary

## Holiday Calendar
- Monthly calendar view with prev/next navigation
- Click any date to add a holiday, event, or note
- Types: Public Holiday (red), Gym Event (blue), Note (yellow)
- Upcoming events sidebar with delete option

## Lead Tracker
- Stages: New Lead → Called → Visited → Enrolled → Lost
- Kanban board view and Table view toggle
- Lead detail modal with stage movement
- Filters: source, interest, search
- Stats: total, enrolled, new leads, follow-up today

## WhatsApp Templates
- 12 built-in templates across 6 categories
- Categories: Welcome, Follow-up, Offer, Renewal, Motivation, Event
- Variables: {name} {date} {phone} {gym} {amount} {plan}
- One-click copy to clipboard
- Create, edit, delete custom templates

## Member Management
- Full CRUD for gym members
- Plan: Monthly (30 days), Quarterly (90 days), Annual (365 days)
- Payment status: Paid / Unpaid / Partial — click to toggle
- Member status: Active / Expiring (≤7 days) / Expired
- Days left counter with color coding
- Assign trainers from staff list
- One-click renew (resets from today)
- Auto WhatsApp renewal messages
- Export CSV

## Dashboard
- Stats: active staff, total leads, enrolled, upcoming events
- Lead Pipeline bar chart
- Lead Sources doughnut chart
- Staff by Role bar chart
- Monthly new leads line chart (6 months)
